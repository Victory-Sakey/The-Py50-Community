Be a part of Our Big Dream!

Each member contributes a significant and indispensable role to the very foundation, growth, and ongoing existence of the community, ensuring its success, vibrancy, and ability to thrive collectively.

Join us with the role that best suits you

Scholar.
As a Scholar in the community, you will have the opportunity to learn a technology of your choice from our selection of 50 core technologies. You’ll receive instruction from our Tutors and be paired with a Mentor to provide additional guidance throughout your learning journey.
Mentor.
As a Mentor in the community, you’ll be assigned a Scholar to mentor and guide individually, enabling you to expand your own knowledge while sharing it with others. This role allows you to make a meaningful impact on their journey while honing your expertise.
Tutor.
As a Tutor in the community, you’ll be responsible for teaching any skills you’re proficient in among our 50 core technologies to Scholars eager to learn. You’ll serve as a leader, becoming a trusted figure that Scholars look up to. Additionally, you’ll collaborate with fellow Tutors to organize and lead impactful projects, fostering both personal and community growth.
